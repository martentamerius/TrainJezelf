Break out! helps you to 'break out' of what you are doing at a given time. You can use this for example to help you learn new habits, return to the present moment, or train your awareness. Essentially, Break out! helps you to remind yourself of something in an intuitive way.

Examples of messages that you could use:

  • don't forget to drink some water
  • what do I need right now?
  • am I doing what I want to do?
  • how do I feel?
  • am I being mild to myself?
  • what do I experience in my body?
